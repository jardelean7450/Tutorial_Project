Queso Frito con Mangu Recipe

Recipe

Ingredients:

  1. -5 green unripened plantains, peeled
  2. -5 cloves of garlic, peeled and sliced
  3. -2 cups of vegetable or chicken stock
  4. -1 cup of water
  5. -2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  6. -3 tablespoons butter
  7. -1 block frying cheese, cut into ¼” slices
  8. -1 large onion, sliced into rings
  9. -salt to taste

Instructions

  1. --Cut plantains on a bias into ¼ inch slices.
  2. -Place in medium sized stock potwith stock and water, and bring to a boil.
  3. -Cover and simmer until plantains are fork-tender.
  4. -Cover and allow to cool slightly.
  5. -In a medium sized bowl, add softened plantains, butter and half of the cooking liquid.
  6. -Mash until all ingredients are combined.
  7. -The mixture should have a slightly wet consistency. Set aside.
  8. -Place vegetable oil in medium skillet and add onions with a pinch of salt.
  9. -Cook until translucent and remove from pan, shaking off any excess oil.
  10. -Fry cheese slices until golden on each side. Remove from pan and set aside.
  11. -Ensure skillet has approximately 1 tablespoon of oil after onions and cheese have been cooked.
  12. -Add garlic to pan and cook until it becomes aromatic (about 1-2 minutes). Don’t allow garlic to brown or burn.
  13. -Add plantain mash to pan and incorporate with garlic oil. Add salt to taste.
  14. -Serve mangu (mashed plantains) with onions and fried cheese. Enjoy!
