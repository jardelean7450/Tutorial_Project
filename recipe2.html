Chimichurri Recipe

Recipe

Ingredients:

  1. -1 1/4 pound ground beef chuck
  2. -1 medium onion, finely chopped
  3. -1/2 large red bell pepper, diced
  4. -2 garlic cloves, minced
  5. -1/3 cup chopped cilantro
  6. -1 teaspoon dried oregano
  7. -2 teaspoons soy sauce
  8. -1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  9. -4 hamburger buns, split
  10. -2 cups thinly sliced cabbage
  11. -1 carrot, coarsely grated
  12. -1 small red onion, cut into rings
  13. -1 tomato, sliced 1/4 inch thick
  14. -2 tablespoons ketchup
  15. -2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  16. -1 tablespoon yellow mustard

Instructions

  1. -Mix together beef, onion, bell pepper, garlic, cilantro, oregano, soy sauce,
  2. Worcestershire sauce, a scant 1/2 teaspoon salt, and 1/2 teaspoon pepper.
  3. Form into 4 (4 1/2-inch-wide) patties.

  4. Heat a large griddle or 12-inch heavy skillet over medium heat until hot, then lightly toast buns.

  5. Oil griddle, then cook patties, turning once, about 8 minutes total for medium-rare. Transfer to buns.

  6. Mix together cabbage, carrot, and 1/4 teaspoon salt, then cook,
  7. turning occasionally, until slightly wilted, about 2 minutes. Divide among burgers.

  8. Oil griddle again, then sear onion and tomato, turning once, until slightly charred, about 2 minutes total. Divide among burgers.

  9. Stir together ketchup, mayonnaise, and mustard, then top burgers with sauce.
