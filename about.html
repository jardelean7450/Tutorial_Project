About This Website

This website will help visitors get a better understand of Dominican, Romanian, and Korean Food Cultures.

The foods that have been selected from Korea include Kongguksu and Daetongbap, which are both vegan dishes.

They have been a part of Korean cuisine for hundreds of years, and are symbols of the Korean diet.

The foods that have been selected for the Dominican Republic are Queso Frito con Mangu and Chimichurri, which are traditional Domincian dishes found all over the Dominican Republic.

The foods that have been selected for Romania include mititei and toltott kaposzta, which both are meat dishes. The dishes are very famous

in Romania, and were foods that Jonathan ate when he lived in Romania.
